Sadaf
Ferdowsi

is a writer based in California and the Midwest. Raised in rural Michigan, she attended the University of Chicago and Columbia College Chicago. Her essay “Albaloo” won a Best of the Net Award and her work can be found in Gordon Square Review, Newcity, The Rumpus, and the 2nd Story podcast. She writes about family, feminism, and Iranian American heritage.
